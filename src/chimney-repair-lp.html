Structural Integrity

What's the Most Common Cause of Chimney Damage?

Water is the number one cause of chimney damage - and in Ohio, it's a year-round threat. Freeze-thaw cycles force water into tiny cracks, expanding them until mortar joints crumble, brick faces spall, and structural integrity is compromised. Without timely repairs, even minor water infiltration can escalate into a full chimney rebuild. Our experts identify and seal every entry point, restoring your chimney's strength and protecting your home from the inside out.

Best Ways to Avoid Chimney Damage

  • Schedule annual professional inspections
  • Install a high-quality chimney cap
  • Burn only seasoned hardwood
  • Invest in exterior chimney waterproofing
  • Clear nearby overhanging tree branches
  • Address damages or missing components quickly
