EXPERT GUIDANCE

The National Standard: What the Experts Recommend

The Chimney Safety Institute of America (CSIA) provides a clear guideline: your chimney should be inspected at least once per year, and swept as needed based on that inspection.

Key Factors That Determine Cleaning Frequency

  • ◆How often you use your fireplace (weekly, occasional, seasonal)
  • ◆The type of fuel you burn (hardwood, softwood, gas, oil)
  • ◆Whether you have a wood-burning stove, fireplace, or both
  • ◆The age and condition of your chimney
  • ◆Local climate and weather patterns in the Columbus area
  • ◆For most homeowners who use their fireplace regularly during the heating season, a professional chimney sweep once per year is the standard recommendation. However, the exact frequency depends on how often you use your fireplace or wood stove, the type of fuel you burn, and the condition of your chimney.
