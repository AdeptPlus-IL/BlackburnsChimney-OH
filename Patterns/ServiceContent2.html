WHY IT MATTERS

What Proper Masonry Repair Does for Your Home

Properly repaired chimney masonry does more than restore appearance. It protects the structure of your home, improves draft performance, and prevents the kind of water infiltration that causes the most expensive chimney repairs.

Stops Water Infiltration

Sealed mortar and a repaired crown block water entry.

Extends Chimney Life

Addressing minor deterioration early saves major costs.

Improves Draft Performance

Intact masonry maintains the correct airflow.

Protects Your Home's Structure

Water that enters through damaged masonry spreads to framing.

Maintains Curb Appeal

Clean, intact brick and mortar keeps the home's appearance.

Prevents Carbon Monoxide Risk

Cracks in masonry can allow combustion gases into the home.
