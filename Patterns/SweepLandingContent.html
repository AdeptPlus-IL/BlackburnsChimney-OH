Why Annual Chimney Sweeps Matter

A clean chimney is essential for the safety and efficiency of your home. Over time, burning wood creates creosote—a highly flammable byproduct that coats the inside of your flue. An annual sweep not only prevents devastating chimney fires but also uncovers hidden structural issues like water damage, failing dampers, or cracked crowns before they become costly repairs.
