How It Works

We take the guesswork out of chimney repairs with a transparent, 5-step process designed for quality and peace of mind.

1. Inspection

We conduct a thorough examination of your chimney's exterior to identify spalling, mortar decay, and structural risks.

2. Assessment

Our certified masons document the extent of the damage and explain the root causes of the masonry failure.

3. Repair Plan

You receive a clear, customized estimate outlining the exact scope of work and the durable materials we'll use.

4. Masonry Work

We carefully remove damaged sections, meticulously match your existing brickwork, and apply weather-resistant mortar.

5. Final Review

A final walkthrough with our team ensures the structure is solid, secure, and fully restored to its original beauty.
