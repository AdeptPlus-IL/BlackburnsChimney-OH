How Blackburns Repairs Chimney Masonry

Every masonry repair starts with a thorough camera-assisted inspection. We identify the full scope of damage before recommending any work — so you only pay for what's actually needed.

Tuckpointing

Removal of deteriorated mortar joints and replacement with fresh mortar.

Crown Repair & Rebuild

The chimney crown takes the most weather exposure. We repair cracks and rebuild when needed.

Brick Replacement

Spalled, cracked, or structurally compromised bricks are removed and replaced.

Waterproofing

Application of a breathable masonry waterproofing treatment that repels water.
