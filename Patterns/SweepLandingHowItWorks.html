How It Works

Our streamlined 5-step process ensures a safe, thorough, and hassle-free experience from start to finish.

1. Schedule

Pick a time that works for you. We offer flexible scheduling and same-week availability.

2. Arrival & Setup

Our team arrives on time, fully equipped, and sets up drop cloths to guarantee a no-mess job.

3. Sweep & Clean

We safely remove dangerous creosote buildup, soot, and debris from your flue and firebox.

4. Inspection

A thorough video or visual inspection of the cap, crown, damper, and interior structure.

5. Report

You receive a detailed health report of your chimney along with any safety recommendations.
